Welcome!


I'm a postdoctoral researcher in the Systems Robotics and Vision Group, in the University of the Balearic Islands (UIB), Spain. I received the B.Sc. degree in Informatics Engineering by the Politechnic University of Calalonia in 2008, the M.Sc. degree in Information and Communication Technologies from the UIB in 2010, and the Ph.d. from the same university in 2017.

My research interests include pattern recognition, mobile robotics and unmanned aerial vehicles. I'm currently researching in visual defect detection using semi-autonomous aerial vehicles.
